Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

Thank you for using MeowLens - Cat Identifier ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The App allows you to upload images from your device's gallery or take photos using your camera. These images are used solely for the purpose of identifying cat breeds and providing app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your images are processed in real time to identify cat breeds.
  • We do not store your images after processing.
  • We do not use your images for commercial purposes.
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or shared by the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services to process images for identification purposes. These services are used only to provide the core functionality of the App and do not store your images after processing.

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement measures to ensure that your images are processed securely and are not retained after use.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
